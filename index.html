About Us

Global Harvest & Revival Ministries

Global Harvest and Revival Ministries is a non-denominational and inter-denominational international ministry to the body of Christ with the mandate of reaching the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ, focusing on soul winning and providing teachings that stir revival, healing, deliverance, and empower people for victorious living.
