Powerful Testimonies

Global Harvest & Revival Ministries

Global Harvest and Revival is a non-denominational ministry dedicated to reaching the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ, focusing on soul winning and providing teachings that stir revival, healing, deliverance and empower people for victorious living. We are dedicated to revival, igniting a fresh hunger for God and stirring spiritual awakening wherever the Lord sends us. Through powerful teachings, prophetic impartation, and Spirit-led gatherings, we emphasize healing, deliverance, and the supernatural works of the Holy Spirit. Our mission is to empower individuals for victorious living, helping them grow in faith, overcome life’s challenges, and become vessels through whom God can touch their families, communities, and the world.
