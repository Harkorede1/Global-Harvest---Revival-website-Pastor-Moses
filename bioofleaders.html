Bio Of Leaders.

The Founder & Pioneer

Evangelist Moses Abimiku

Evangelist Moses Abimiku is a servant of God with a burning passion for soul winning and igniting revival in the hearts of people. In 2017, he pioneered a citywide outreach movement in North Cyprus, where the power of God was demonstrated through remarkable miracles, healings, deliverances, salvations, and revival that impacted the nation. His evangelistic mission has since extended beyond Cyprus into Uganda, Kenya, and Nigeria, where multitudes have been reached with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Through his ministry, thousands have received salvation, and countless testimonies of miracles, healings, and deliverances continue to abound. He is divinely mandated to raise end-time disciples of Jesus Christ, carrying the spirit of revival and equipping believers for the effective work of ministry. The vision of Global Harvest and Revival draws great inspiration from the ministries of Evangelist Reinhard Bonnke, Billy Graham, and Dr. Rodney Howard-Browne, who have impacted the world through mass crusades and the preaching of the Gospel.

Head Coord. for Global Crusades,
Outreaches & Conference

Dr. Oye Lemoru

Dr. Oye Lemoru, DNP, FNP-C, PMHNP-BC, is a servant of Christ with a passion for helping people walk in healing, wholeness, and purpose. As both a Family and Psychiatric Mental Health Practitioner, she integrates her professional expertise with her faith to minister to the whole person; spirit, soul, and body. With a heart for serving and empowering others, her mission is to bring hope, restoration, and lasting transformation through the love of Christ.

Head Coordinator for Youth & Campus Outreaches

Jahnelle Bigby

Jahnelle Bigby is a powerful testament to the transformative power of Christ. Once lost and broken, she was surrounded by darkness, only to be rescued by the love and light of Jesus. Today, she shines brightly as a beacon of hope in the healthcare industry as a PCA, using her gifts to care for those in need. With a heart that still remembers the depths of her own struggles, Jahnelle approaches her work with compassion, empathy, and a deep understanding of the human experience. Her passion for serving others is fueled by the words of Isaiah 61:1, and she strives to bring healing, hope, and joy to all those she touches. As she lives out her faith with boldness and conviction, Jahnelle is a beautiful reflection of God's redeeming love.

Carbrini Egbo chosen by God, walking in faith and truth, with a passion to see lives healed, restored, and set free. Her calling is to speak life, and move with compassion so that Christ alone is glorified. She lives to be a light in dark places and a witness of God’s power and love.
